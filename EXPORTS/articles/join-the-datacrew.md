DataCrew is in its infancy, but we are excited to grow this community and bring you high-quality long-form content that will allow you to advance your career in data either as a developer, a consultant, or a strategic thinker.

Most of our early content will be Domo specific, but we have plans to expand to encompass data science, data engineering, data governance, SQL and BI, and app development training programs.

## Why should you Register?

- Stay abreast with the latest news with a weekly newsletter digest of what's happening in Domo.

- Gain access to exclusive webinars and events.  Join our exclusive Slack community.

## Register for 3 Months Free Access

To get the community off the ground, we are offering 3 months of access for [free](https://datacrew.memberspace.com/member/plans/bc57004f7p).
