Not every consulting engagement has to be a Domo-styled "Momentum" implementation.

On the heels of our "[Becoming a Better Consultant](https://datacrew.circle.so/c/events/becoming-a-better-consultant)" series, we made a list of services data consultants could offer.

Strategic planning

- Value roadmap

- QBR (quarterly business review)

- Solution design workshop

Implementation

- Deployment

Standard Reporting

- Writeback & systems integration

- Custom App (pure viz and/or data interaction)

- Data Science & advanced analytics workflow

- Data Governance Workshop

- Project manager

Adoption

- Domo Immersion

- Certifications Training

- 1:1 Mentoring & Coaching

Ongoing Program

- COE Launch Program

- Fractional

Major Domo

- Pipeline Engineer

- DevOps developer
