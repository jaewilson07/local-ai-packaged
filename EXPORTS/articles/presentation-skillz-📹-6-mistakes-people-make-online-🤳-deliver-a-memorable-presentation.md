## Domo IDEA Exchange Conference Prep (Mastermind)

As preparation for the Spring Domo IDEA Exchange conference, we hosted an hour-long mastermind session where we discussed understanding your audience, managing your time, and collaborating with strangers.

### Why are you presenting?

- Who is you audience // what are their goals?

- What is the audience takeaway?

### Time

Pace yourself // how long till you get to the stuff people care about?

### Agenda

- Respect the audience's time. provide an agenda

- Preview your content / what's the benefit?
