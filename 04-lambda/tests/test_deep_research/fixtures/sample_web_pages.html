Deep Research Methodology

Introduction

Deep research is a comprehensive methodology for investigating complex topics through systematic investigation and analysis.

Key Principles

  • Systematic investigation
  • Multiple source verification
  • Comprehensive analysis
  • Evidence-based conclusions

Research Process

The deep research process involves several key steps:

  1. Question formulation
  2. Source identification
  3. Data collection
  4. Analysis and synthesis
  5. Report generation

Conclusion

Deep research provides a structured approach to understanding complex topics through thorough investigation.
